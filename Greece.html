About Greece

Greece is located to the south by the Mediterranian sea and has more than 2000 islands. Greece is known for lots of popular food dishes such as Loukoumades. They also have festivals and celebrations including Navy Week to celebrate the island's maritime by swimming, dancing, playing music and sailing in the ocean. Greece has plenty of scenic views of the coast and my favorite part about Greece is visiting the Greek Islands. Here's a beautiful picture of Greece for you.
