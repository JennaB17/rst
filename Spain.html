About Spain

Spain is a country located in Southwestern Europe. It is known for their culture, amazing food and the scenery. There are so many celebrations and festivals to witness and be a part of in Spain. An example is the Feria De Malaga. In my opinion Spain is a country that everyone should visit because there is so much that you can do everyday, you can enjoy the beautiful coastlines go watch a soccer game and relax on the beaches.

Major Cities To Visit

1. Barcelona


2. Madrid


3. Palma de Mallorca


Tourist Attractions

Here are some fun activities that you can do while visiting Spain.


1. Visit the Palacio Real in Madrid


2. Watch the Running of the Bulls from july 6 - 14 in Pamplona


3. Relax on La Concha Beach in San Sebastian


4. See the Sagrada Familia church in Barcelona


5. Visit the Cathedral de Mallorca in Palma de Mallorca
