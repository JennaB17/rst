About Monaco

Monaco is a country located between France, Italy and the Mediterranian sea. Monaco reflects the Mediterranian heritage and both Italian, French and sea foods. You can find so many expensive products here. There are so many mind-blowing scenic views. In addition, there are a lot of celebrities that live in Monaco. I loved travelling to Monaco because of the great food and cliff views. I also liked seeing all the expensive cars and tourist attractions.

Tourist Attractions

2. Walk to the Cathedral de Monaco


3. Go to Monte Carlo Harbor


4. Go see the Prince's Palace of Monaco
